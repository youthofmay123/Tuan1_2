A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscle at the sides of the mounth.It is belived it take about 80 muscles to smile.Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchene smile".Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
